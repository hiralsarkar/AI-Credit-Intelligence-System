Investment Thesis

The current Aggressive policy places 60.5% of the approved book in Band 4 (High Risk, RAROC -20.9%), producing a portfolio RAROC of -7.1% and net income of -₹169M on 28,079 approved loans.

The proposal reallocates lending away from economically destructive exposures and toward risk-adjusted profitable segments while preserving regulatory compliance and portfolio resilience. This converts the ₹169M loss into a ₹166M profit, reduces expected defaults by 87%, and lifts RAROC from -7.1% to +36.2%.

Strategic Insight

Value creation is driven primarily by removing the economically destructive Band 4 segment rather than by improving model discrimination. The strategy works because of capital allocation discipline, not algorithmic complexity.
