Investment Thesis

The current Aggressive policy approves all applicants with PD below 35%, placing 60.5% of the approved book in Band 4 (High Risk, RAROC -20.9%). The result: portfolio RAROC of -7.1% and a net income loss of -₹169M on 28,079 approved loans. The Risk Segmentation Framework applies a RAROC-gated approval policy, eliminating all Band 4 exposure. This converts the ₹169M loss into a ₹166M profit, reduces defaults by 87% from 737 to 96, and lifts portfolio RAROC from -7.1% to +36.2% - satisfying the SC3 Gold Standard: fewer approvals AND lower default rate simultaneously.
