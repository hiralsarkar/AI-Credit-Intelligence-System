Investment Thesis

The current production framework is operating outside its design envelope — provision expense has increased ₹74M year-on-year, RAROC has fallen below the 15% hurdle, and population drift is approaching amber. This proposal reallocates ₹36.6B of lending exposure toward a more profitable and capital-efficient risk profile through an Enhanced Credit Strategy, estimated to generate ₹43M incremental annual economic profit (95% CI: ₹36M–₹51M) while maintaining full regulatory compliance and operational control.
